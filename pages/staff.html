See our staff's favorite books from this month

Our staff were quickly enamoured by the intriguing Kaz Brekker and his crew of thieves. Follow this band on their epic heist filled with adrenaline and witty one-liners! Leigh Bardugo's exceptional world-building and character dynamics will keep you engaged page after page. You can't go wrong with the Six of Crows. Keep an eye out for its sequel, Crooked Kingdom, which should join our stock soon.

The Deepest Blue became one of our favorites from the earliest pages. We couldn't resist the vibrant characters, their emotional journeys, and the well-established world around them. It was impossible not to be drawn into the stakes of the characters' decisions, especially when all the odds were against them. Sarah Beth Durst expands on her world of Renthia in this magnificent novel, which we hope you take the time to read!

A timeless favorite, Shannon Hale's tale of a princess turned goose tender is like coming home. With betrayals, reunions, a love story, magic, and complicated family relationships, The Goose Girl truly has something for everyone. Do yourself a favor and sink into the warm embrace of this tale!
